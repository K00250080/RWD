AC Valhalla: List of weapon locations and abilities

This page contains the locations and abilities of all known weapons (including one-handed, two-handed, bows and shields) in AC Valhalla.

Unlike other Assassin's Creed games, weapons in AC Valhalla are very unique, and not many can be found in the world. Each weapon has its own passive ability, and its stats can be upgraded with certain materials. Additionally, weapons can be enhanced with collectible ingots to add rune slots, upgrade their look, and the amount of times they can be upgraded.
